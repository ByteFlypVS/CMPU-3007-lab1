An Overview Of Task Management & To-Do Lists

Task management and to-do lists have evolved over centuries. In ancient times, people used simple reminders like knots in ropes or inscriptions on tablets. In the 20th century, paper-based to-do lists gained popularity, with productivity experts like Ivy Lee and Alan Lakein promoting their use. The digital age brought a shift to software-based task management tools, starting with early personal computer software and advancing with the proliferation of smartphones. Today, task management apps and cloud-based solutions have become integral for organizing and prioritizing daily tasks, ensuring efficiency in both personal and professional life.
